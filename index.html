About ChalegaOn

Where Every Stay Tells a Story

Founded in the bylanes of Pune in 2018, ChalegaOn was born from a simple belief — that the most beautiful experiences in travel happen not in five-star hotels, but in a farmer's hut, a grandmother's kitchen, or a misty hilltop shack.

We partner with local families across India to offer you genuine homestays that support village economies and create unforgettable memories. "Chalegaon" — let's go to the village.
